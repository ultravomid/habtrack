🪜 The Drilling Crew Ladder
Standard offshore progression. Starting at the bottom is fine — field competence earns promotion, and certifications accelerate every step.
1
Roustabout
Entry, no experience needed. Deck support, equipment moves. ~1–2 yrs.
2
Roughneck / Floorhand
Works the drill floor, handles pipe. ~2–3 yrs.
3
Derrickman
Works at height, manages pipe racking & fluids.
4
Assistant Driller → Driller
Operates & supervises the floor. ~5–8 yrs from entry.
5
Toolpusher → OIM → Rig Manager
Runs the rig. ~8–12 yrs field time.
⚡ Your shortcut: skilled mechanical/marine trades + IWCF shorten the early stages. Better yet, enter on the maintenance/mechanical track (Rig Mechanic → Senior Mechanic → Maintenance Supervisor) — it matches your CV far more directly.
